ASMRtists

A print fulfillment and NFT portal that turns visual art into on-chain revenue — for artists, collectors, and curators — built on the BSV Blockchain.

ASMRtists (Art Splash Marketing Roster) is a platform designed to give visual artists a novel, scalable way to monetize their work. Artists upload high-resolution images; the platform handles everything else — automated print shop creation, BSV Ordinal inscription, and a revenue-share system that pays collectors when prints sell.

The core innovation: when a collector purchases an Ordinal (a JPEG permanently inscribed on the BSV Blockchain), they gain a share of future print sale revenue from that artwork. The Ordinal is not just a collectible — it's a revenue-sharing artifact. Holders become economically motivated to promote the work they believe in.

The platform grew directly out of Ordinal Rainbows Vol.1 at ordinalrainbows.com — a fully live proof-of-concept with 63 on-chain inscriptions, real holders, and a working Printify print shop. That working system became the design foundation; ASMRtists scales it into a multi-artist, investor-ready platform explicitly designed for acquisition.

Four Portals

Artists

Upload artwork, create collections, and hand off — ASMR handles print fulfillment and blockchain inscription automatically.

Collectors

Mint Ordinals, buy prints, and earn MNEE stablecoin payouts when prints of held artwork sell.

Curators

Gallery-tier curation tools for institutions and influencers to build and manage themed showrooms.

Gallery

Publicly browsable marketplace with featured artists, collector leaderboards, and seamless purchase prompts.

How It Works

  • Artists upload high-resolution PNGs; the platform auto-generates three Printify product types (canvas, art print, photo print)
  • Files are converted and inscribed as BSV 1Sat Ordinals via an in-house minter built on @bsv/sdk, broadcast via TAAL ARC
  • An equitable revenue split routes earnings to the artist, holder pool, and platform on every print sale
  • Payouts are distributed in MNEE stablecoin, preserving USD value regardless of BSV price movement

Tech Stack

  • Next.js (App Router) frontend with shadcn/ui — deployed on Vercel
  • Supabase (PostgreSQL + Storage) — full schema covering artists, collections, artwork, payouts, and claims
  • BSV 1Sat Ordinals in-house minter — complete, using @bsv/sdk with TAAL ARC broadcast
  • Printify API integration — automated product creation pipeline via Python
  • MNEE stablecoin treasury for collector revenue share; Stripe Connect for artist fiat payouts
  • In-house 1Sat Ordinals marketplace — ordinals list via on-chain locking scripts; purchases fulfill atomically in a single transaction with no custodian and no counterparty risk
