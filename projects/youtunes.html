YouTunes

A music streaming platform built on the BSV Blockchain — where every stream sends a direct micropayment to the artist, with no intermediary taking a cut.

YouTunes reimagines the economics of music streaming. The dominant platforms pay artists fractions of a cent per stream while capturing the vast majority of revenue generated by their work. YouTunes flips that model by using the BSV Blockchain to route micropayments directly to artists every time their music is played — transparently, instantly, and without middlemen.

Built on BSV's high-throughput, low-fee infrastructure, the platform makes per-stream payments economically viable at scale. Every transaction is recorded on-chain, giving artists full visibility into their earnings and listeners a verifiable record of their support. The result is a streaming service where the value of music flows directly back to the people who create it.

What It Does

  • Per-stream micropayments sent directly to artists via BSV Blockchain
  • Transparent, on-chain payment records accessible to both artists and listeners
  • No record labels, no intermediaries — artists upload and earn directly
  • Decentralized music catalog resistant to censorship and platform risk
  • Low transaction fees enabled by BSV's scalable blockchain architecture
  • Artist dashboard with real-time earnings tracking and stream analytics
