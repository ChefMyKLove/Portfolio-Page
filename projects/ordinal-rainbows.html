Ordinal Rainbows

A digital art ecosystem built around rainbow photography — minting original works as permanent, on-chain Ordinal NFTs on the BSV Blockchain.

Ordinal Rainbows is a digital art project and ecosystem built around the beauty and symbolism of rainbow photography. The work uses the BSV Blockchain's Ordinals protocol to inscribe original photographs directly onto the blockchain — creating immutable, permanent records of each piece that exist independently of any centralized platform or server.

The project bridges fine art photography, Web3 infrastructure, and print-on-demand commerce. Collectors can own authenticated digital originals with verifiable on-chain provenance, while also ordering physical prints through the integrated Printify storefront. Each piece in the collection is a unique capture of naturally occurring rainbow light — no filters, no AI generation, just light and a lens.

What It Does

  • Ordinal NFT minting platform inscribing artwork directly onto BSV Blockchain
  • Interactive gallery and collection explorer with full artwork details
  • Print-on-demand integration for ordering physical prints via Printify
  • Immutable on-chain provenance records for every piece in the collection
  • Original rainbow photography by Michael Needham — naturally captured, unfiltered
  • Educational content on BSV Blockchain, Ordinals protocol, and digital ownership
