Stratos

A real-time global systems monitor aggregating live data on weather patterns, seismic activity, and space weather into a single unified dashboard.

Stratos brings together the planet's most critical monitoring networks into one place. Weather systems, earthquake activity, and space weather — these are interconnected forces that affect billions of people, yet the data that tracks them is scattered across dozens of agencies, formats, and interfaces that most people can't navigate.

The platform is designed for researchers, emergency preparedness communities, aurora chasers, amateur scientists, and anyone who wants to understand what the planet is doing in real time. Stratos takes complex Earth systems data and makes it accessible, visual, and actionable — from a magnitude 6.2 quake off the Pacific coast to an incoming geomagnetic storm from a solar flare.

What It Does

  • Live global weather tracking with aggregated meteorological data feeds
  • Real-time seismic activity monitoring with earthquake alerts and magnitude maps
  • Space weather dashboard tracking solar flares, geomagnetic storms, and aurora forecasts
  • Historical data visualization and trend analysis across all monitored systems
  • Configurable alerts for significant events by region, magnitude, or severity
  • Clean, unified interface that requires no scientific background to navigate
