ORDINAL 🌈 RAINBOWS

Original rainbow photography, hand-manipulated and inscribed as 1sat Ordinals on the BSV Blockchain.

Project Overview

Art on Chain

The Collection

Ordinal Rainbows Vol. 1 is 63 original artifacts permanently inscribed on the BSV Blockchain. Each piece starts as a real photograph — prisms, atmospheric refractions, light caught in unexpected places — then worked by hand through colour grading, compositing, and layering. No AI at any stage. The collection is fully minted out with around 15 holders.

The Novel Mechanic

Holders earn automatically. When the physical print of an artifact you hold sells, the revenue is distributed on-chain — connect your BSV wallet to claim it. The same logic extends to the GIF minter launching soon: every time a GIF ordinal is minted from your artifact, you earn. Holders are also positioned for royalties as those GIFs trade. The artifacts are used as source material in other on-chain projects in development too, each one another revenue channel flowing back to holders of the originals.
